6. PURPOSE OF A TOKEN:

OPL token which we offer during TGE is a currency. There’s going to be two type of tokens. The first one is a utility token, which is the one that pre-sale participants can purchase on February 1. This token will be circulating on different exchanges, it is the domestic currency for the OnPlace ecosystem. The second type of token will be a security token, this token will be backed by shares of private companies. The only way to purchase a security token is through OPL. OnPlace will be the legal private asset holder, and security token holders will be members of participation.

8. HOW OnPlace TOKEN HOLDER WILL BENEFIT?

Tokens are tied up to capitalization growth of private shares. There will be different cases with different rules. But for the most part we will hold on to private equity until a major event occurs, for example, the private company goes to IPO, or is being bought out by VC monopolists. That’s when the closing time will occur, payout revenue will be spread among token holders equal to the amount of their tokens. For those who don’t want to wait for the closing period: Tokenized shares will be circulating in private Exchange, where you can trade it and come out of the deal earlier.
